## Voice Guidelines
- First person (I / we)
- Confident but not arrogant
- Show don't tell — demonstrate AI capabilities
- Short sentences. Punchy paragraphs.
- No emojis in every line — use sparingly
- No hashtags on X (cringe). 2-3 on LinkedIn max.
- Controversial takes welcome — they drive engagement
